How To Play

SIMPLY SCORE AS MANY POINTS AS YOU
 can eating the small dots all around
the maze. 10 points per dot (240 of
them). Big points come when you eat 1
of the 4 Big flashing dots called
Energizers worth 50 points located in
each corner of the maze.
